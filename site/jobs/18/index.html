Inside Sales Rep For National Real Estate Company

US Landvestors · Remote, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $40K – $250K
InsideImmediate

If you’re a winner, I am talking to you. If you’re anything less than stellar, please stop reading.

My name is Stephen Kiser and I am the CEO of Favored Home Buyers. We are a growing national house buying company located in the state of North Carolina. If you find yourself under\-utilized, under\-trained, under\-challenged, and you want to run towards an amazing opportunity, read on.

I’m talking $40K \- $275K a year working out of your home office \+ bonuses for performance. If you find yourself being “The BEST” at whatever you do, I’m speaking to you. Don’t waste your time if your just looking for another job, this isn’t it. This is an opportunity to FINALLY make the money that matches your work ethic, talent, and desire to succeed.

Our company currently buys between 7\-10 houses a month and we do it remotely. That’s right, we buy houses right over the telephone with homeowners who need a real solution to a real estate problem. You will be assisting the company in the acquisition of the property, working directly with sellers

If you have what it takes, you will focus only on the 80/20, that’s the 20% of activity that will give you 80% of your results. Your commute? Gone. Useless meetings? Gone. Bureaucracy and sensitivity training? Not on our team. This place is different. It’s awesome. Forget about being micromanaged. Attack the leads, nurture them, convert, and you will have the ultimate combination of freedom and income.

Our team is here to pour leadership, knowledge, love, and gratitude to our group. You will be trained by the top acquisitions rep personally, along with a slew of the best training programs known to this industry. You will have support from the top acquisitions rep whenever you need help closing a deal. You will get a database of leads to start calling immediately, plus fresh HOT leads delivered to you every day. You will even have appointments set for you, once your prove yourself.

Sound Awesome? Good. Let’s see if you have what it takes. I’m taking on 1 WINNER immediately. You don’t need house buying, real estate, or sales experience. But don’t even think about applying unless you have a habit outshining everybody everywhere you go.

A couple of requirements: This is full\-time. You must have a dedicated home office free of distractions and noise. You must be committed to bettering yourself every day. You must be accountable and hold others accountable. You must radiate gratitude and seize the opportunity. You must be open to being mentored and mentoring others. Finally, you must have courage, remember you're a WINNER, and winners win.

***If you interested in applying send a quick 1\-2 min audio recording letting us know why you feel you would be a good fit for the position at sherwin@favoredhomebuyers.com***

Job Type: Full\-time

Pay: $40,000\.00 \- $250,000\.00 per year

Work Location: Remote
