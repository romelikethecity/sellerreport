Director of Business Development - Commercial Roofing

CES Construction Services · Palm Harbor, FL, US Remote Director
Salary Range: $90K – $275K
Growth HireEquity
Tools: Linkedin Sales Navigator

**Director of Business Development**

CES Roofing is seeking a **Director of Business Development** to lead strategic growth, develop new commercial roofing opportunities, build long\-term relationships with key decision\-makers, and help expand the company’s presence throughout Central and Southwest Florida.

This is a sales role and ideal for a highly connected, high energy, relationship\-driven professional with strong sales instincts, commercial real estate or roofing/construction experience, and the ability to create opportunities through networking, outreach, referrals, industry events, and strategic partnerships.

The Director of Business Development will focus on generating and nurturing commercial relationships with property managers, building owners, facility managers, HOA/condo associations, consultants, asset managers, and other decision\-makers. This role is responsible for opening doors, developing trust, identifying roofing needs, and helping move opportunities into the sales pipeline.

Key ResponsibilitiesBusiness Development \& Relationship Building

* Proactively identify, pursue, and develop new commercial roofing opportunities through networking, referrals, cold outreach, LinkedIn, industry events, association involvement, and strategic relationship building.
* Build and maintain strong relationships with property managers, property owners, facility managers, asset managers, consultants, contractors, HOA/condo board members, and commercial real estate professionals.
* Represent CES Roofing at industry events, networking functions, trade associations, property management events, and community/business development opportunities.
* Develop long\-term referral sources and strategic partnerships that generate consistent commercial roofing opportunities.
* Promote CES Roofing’s capabilities, including commercial roof repairs, replacements, coatings, SPF, TPO/PVC, modified bitumen, metal roofing, preventative maintenance, roof inspections, storm response, and roof asset management.

Sales Pipeline Development

* Generate qualified leads and new opportunities for commercial roof inspections, repairs, maintenance programs, restoration, replacement, and storm\-related services.
* Work closely with estimators, project managers, and ownership to move qualified opportunities through the sales process.
* Maintain consistent follow\-up with prospects, open opportunities, past clients, and referral partners.
* Track outreach activity, prospect conversations, follow\-up dates, opportunities, and relationship status in the company CRM or designated tracking system.
* Help identify stalled proposals, older opportunities, and past contacts that may be ready to re\-engage.
* Support the development of account\-specific strategies for large property portfolios, management companies, commercial owners, and high\-value prospects.

Client Strategy \& Market Growth

* Identify target accounts, property management companies, commercial property portfolios, and key decision\-makers in CES Roofing’s service areas.
* Create opportunities for lunch\-and\-learns, educational events, roof assessment campaigns, hurricane preparedness outreach, maintenance program discussions, and other relationship\-building initiatives.

Required Qualifications

* Minimum of 5 years of proven business development, outside sales, account management, or relationship\-based sales experience.
* Experience in closing large contracts.
* Strong ability to build trust with property managers, owners, facility managers, and commercial decision\-makers.
* Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
* Professional presence and comfort representing the company at networking events, client meetings, and industry functions.
* Strong follow\-up, organization, and time\-management skills.
* Ability to manage multiple prospects, relationships, and opportunities at different stages of the pipeline.
* Self\-motivated and comfortable working independently while staying accountable to company goals.
* Valid state\-issued driver’s license with a clean driving record.

Preferred Qualifications

* Existing network of property managers, building owners, facility managers, HOA/condo association contacts, commercial real estate professionals, contractors, or consultants in Central and Southwest Florida.
* Knowledge of commercial roofing systems, roof repairs, roof coatings, SPF, TPO/PVC, modified bitumen, metal roofing, and preventative maintenance programs.
* Experience selling to property management companies, commercial portfolios, multifamily communities, condominiums, office buildings, retail centers, industrial properties, or institutional facilities.
* Experience using CRM systems, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, lead generation platforms, or sales tracking tools.
* Existing involvement in organizations such as BOMA, IREM, CAI, CREW, local chambers, or other commercial real estate/property management associations.
* Bilingual English/Spanish is a plus.

Pay: $90,000\.00 \- $275,000\.00 per year

Benefits:

* Employee assistance program
* Health insurance
* Paid time off
* Professional development assistance

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Palm Harbor, FL
