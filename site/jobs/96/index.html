Account Executive, ENT Customer

Trimble · CO, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $190K – $200K
EnterpriseEnterprise DealLongNorth AmericaGlobalBuild TeamTurnaroundEquity
Tools: Salesforce

Drive Digital Transformation as our next Enterprise Account Executive!

Are you a professional sales representative interested in working with disruptive cutting\-edge technologies? Join our team as an Account Executive and empower our customers to drive productivity and progress with connected hardware and software solutions that transform the way the world works.

**What Makes This Role Great:**

In this role, you’ll be at the forefront of the AECO industry, directly influencing the digital evolution of our enterprise partners by expanding our footprint and delivering ERP solutions that increase precision and productivity for Architecture, Engineering, Construction, and Operations.

**Key Exciting Responsibilities**

* **Architect and execute strategic territory plans** to expand our footprint within a diverse portfolio of existing enterprise accounts.
* **Consult with industry leaders** to uncover deep business needs and deliver high\-impact ERP solutions that solve critical operational challenges.
* **Spearhead the full sales lifecycle**, from identifying fresh pipeline opportunities to negotiating and closing complex enterprise deals.

\+1

* **Collaborate with elite product and marketing teams** to stay ahead of industry trends and provide customers with world\-class product enhancements.
* **Shape the future of construction technology** by providing accurate sales forecasts and results that drive our global business strategy.

**Essential Skills \& Experience**

* You have 5\+ years of proven success selling SaaS solutions, specifically within the ERP landscape.

* You possess a consistent track record of meeting or smashing annual sales quotas at the enterprise level.

* You are a master communicator, capable of delivering exemplary presentations that resonate with stakeholders at all organizational levels.

* You demonstrate advanced negotiation skills and a commitment to long\-term customer relationship strategy.
* You are proficient in Salesforce.com to manage complex sales cycles and reporting.

**Bonus Points For:**

* Deep domain expertise or prior experience within the AECO (Architecture, Engineering, Construction, and Operations) industry.
* Previous experience navigating complex, multi\-stakeholder "land and expand" strategies within large enterprise organizations.

**Logistics**

* **Location:** Remote Mid\-Western or East Coast, USA (Ideally MI, OH, VA, MD, DC, DE)
* **Travel Requirement:** 25%
* **Compensation**: 190k\-200k Salary\+commission

**Why You'll Love Working With Us**

At Trimble, we're not just a company that "does good"—we are a team dedicated to making a tangible, positive **Real\-World Impact**. We build innovative solutions designed to solve the world's most critical challenges. From construction sites to transportation hubs, our work tangibly improves how people live, build, move, and grow. You'll **work on projects that truly matter**: Our purpose\-driven culture means you'll be helping to build and deliver solutions that make work faster, safer, and more sustainable for millions of people worldwide. Our impact is tangible, from connected machines that save fuel to data\-driven insights that reduce waste. **Collaborate with like\-minded people**: Our strong internal culture is a "hidden gem." You will work with a collaborative, supportive team that shares your purpose and fosters a genuine sense of belonging. We're a company of **"visionary pragmatists"** who think boldly and build things that work. **Be an owner**: Trimble thrives on individuals who take initiative and embrace ownership. You'll find an entrepreneurial spirit where success is often "self\-authored," empowering proactive "doers."
