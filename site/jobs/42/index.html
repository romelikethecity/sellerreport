Automotive Sales Manager

Automotive Avenues LLC · Township of Wall, NJ, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $180K – $275K
ImmediateGrowth HireEquity

Automotive Avenues, New Jersey's highest volume car dealership, has an immediate opening for an Automotive Sales Manager to lead our Sales Team. At Automotive Avenues, we stock over 1,000 late\-model vehicles, receive over 5000 sales leads per month, and consistently sell in excess of 700 units per month.

**What we expect:**


3\+ years of Automotive Sales Management experience.


The ability to lead a team of experienced Sales Professionals and conduct training.


Excellent communication skills \& attention to detail.


Conflict management skills


Premier Customer Service

**What we offer:**


An extremely competitive commission\-based pay plan.


Tremendous opportunity for future income \& career growth.


Medical


Paid vacation \& Personal Days


401K with match


If you're an accomplished Automotive Sales Professional who's capable of working in an extremely busy environment, and who takes pride in being the best, this is the opportunity you've been looking for. Respectfully, we ask that only the highly skilled and highly motivated apply! All applications will be handled with total confidentially. Automotive Avenues is an equal opportunity employer.
