Auto Regional Sales Manager

Digi Solutions · Remote, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $120K – $300K

High‑performing automotive closers know the difference between a “job” and a \*\*vehicle\*\*.

If you’re the person everyone calls when a deal is on the line, and you’re tired of carrying teams that don’t match your pace or ambition, this might be the last sales ad you answer.

\#\# Position: Remote Automotive High‑Ticket Sales Professional

\*\*Location:\*\* 100% Remote (US\-based)

\#\#\# The Opportunity

We’re looking for a top‑tier \*\*automotive sales professional\*\* to join a remote, high‑ticket environment where:

\- Every conversation is with qualified, high‑intent automotive buyers or decision‑makers

\- You’re trusted to run a professional, consultative sales process — not just “take ups”

\- Your ceiling is defined only by your ability to perform, not by a comp cap

This role is built for an automotive closer who wants the \*\*real\*\* upside that comes with driving meaningful revenue. Top performers in this seat have the earning potential to reach as high as \*\*$300,000\*\* in total compensation through commissions and performance incentives.

\#\#\# Who You Are

\- Proven closer in \*\*automotive\*\* or related high‑ticket sales (retail, BDC to showroom, digital retailing, F\&I, or B2B automotive services)

\- Thinks in terms of leads, appointments, show rate, and close rate — and knows your numbers cold

\- Comfortable speaking with dealer principals, GMs, and high‑net‑worth clients or decision‑makers

\- Holds yourself to a higher standard than any manager ever has

\- Self‑disciplined and effective working remotely with structured follow‑up and CRM usage

If you’ve led the board, built a strong personal following, turned underperforming internet/BDC channels into profit centers, or driven serious gross month after month, you’ll feel at home here.

\#\#\# What You’ll Do

\- Run a consultative, needs‑based sales process from first contact to close (fully remote)

\- Manage a robust pipeline of automotive prospects and accounts with accurate forecasting

\- Build long‑term relationships that generate repeat, referral, and portfolio business

\- Collaborate with leadership on messaging, offers, and market approach tailored to the automotive space

\- Use CRM tools daily to track activities, opportunities, and outcomes with discipline

You’ll have the tools, support, and autonomy you need — and clear, transparent metrics so you always know exactly where you stand.

\#\#\# Why This Is Different

This is not:

\- A store or team where average performance is tolerated and protected

\- A role where your income is limited by someone else’s comfort level

This \*is\* a home for an \*\*automotive closer\*\* who wants their effort and skill to translate directly into life‑changing income and long‑term opportunity, while working remotely.

\*\*\*

\#\#\# How to Apply

If you’re currently in the top 10% at your dealership or automotive sales role but know there’s more on the table — more impact, more responsibility, and significantly more upside — apply with:

\- A brief summary of your best year in automotive sales (units, gross, or revenue)

\- The types of automotive deals or products you are closing today

\- A short note on why you thrive in a remote, high‑accountability, performance‑driven environment

Only serious, proven automotive high performers should apply.

Pay: $120,000\.00 \- $300,000\.00 per year

Benefits:

* Employee discount
* Flexible schedule
* Referral program

Work Location: Remote
