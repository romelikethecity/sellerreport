Enterprise Account Executive

Localstack · US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $200K – $300K
MeddicEnterpriseFortune 500Enterprise DealSeven FigureLongEmeaGlobalBuild TeamGrowth HireEquityUncappedOte Mentioned
Tools: Docker

We are a fast\-growing Series A startup building cutting\-edge technology to revolutionize cloud development processes and support highly efficient dev\&test feedback loops. We’ve closed our last $25mil round in Q4 2024, led by Notable Capital, CRV and Heavybit.


At its core, LocalStack provides a high\-fidelity emulator and local cloud development platform. Imagine developing cloud applications and data pipelines entirely on your local machine within a lightweight cloud sandbox, running in Docker!


Our **mission** is to empower developers to rapidly build and test their cloud applications, allowing for a more enjoyable dev experience, and saving valuable time and resources.


LocalStack has a large and active open\-source community (57k\+ stars on GitHub) with over 100k active users worldwide and 290M\+ downloads to date. Our customer base ranges from SMBs to Global Fortune 500 companies.


We are sustainably growing our globally distributed team across sectors


LocalStack is headquartered in Zurich/Switzerland , with a main engineering office in Vienna/Austria and remote team members from the US, FR, UK, CA, ES, and many more countries.


Check our Notion Candidate Handbook and our GitHub!

**This is the right opportunity for an experienced senior Enterprise Account Executive with experience in selling to a technical user (both developer and VP/C level leadership) in devtooling or adjacent domains and strong background in enterprise sales.**

**Great opportunity for someone who wants to join a rapidly growing startup post PMF, with strong inbound traction, and uncapped commission structure!**

**Requirements**

✅ What you will be working on/responsible for

* Source and close deals on the Enterprise tier beyond small lands to “try it out first”
* Develop and cultivate deep relationships with senior executives and the end user (software engineers), driving large enterprise deals (incl new business, expansion and upsell)
* Serve as trusted advisor to existing and potential enterprise clients, understanding their needs and advising on the benefits of our offering
* Work closely with product and engineering team to translate feedback and market insights into features and new product offerings


✅ Experience we expect you to bring to the role

* Experience of working on complex enterprise deals within series A\-D environment
* Experience selling to a technical user (security, platform, SRE engineers etc) as well as to a higher decision maker (VP, C\-level)
* Experience working with inbound, outbound and land \& expand motion
* Experience closing (NNARR) 400k\+/ quarter
* Experience working with MEDDPICC methodology

**Benefits**

* Fully remote
* Flexible hours
* Competitive salary
* **Uncapped** commission
* Potential for equity
* Unlimited PTO
* Annual company retreat
* 2 extra company\-wide holidays
* 401k and private medical
* Learning budget

*For US based candidates: The estimated OTE for this role ranges from $200,000 \- $300,000 based on experience and location, along with potential for equity component and a benefits package. Actual compensation may deviate from this range based on skills, experience, and qualifications.*
