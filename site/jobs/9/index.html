Sales Manager, 3D Print

NCS Technologies, Inc. · Manassas, VA, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $100K – $500K
EnterpriseChannelInsideLongEquity

We are currently seeking a Sales Manager, 3D Print, to support and grow our additive manufacturing equipment and services business. Our company is very enthusiastic in the adoption and promotion of additive manufacturing technologies to simplify, strengthen, and bring responsiveness to the supply chain. NCS will rely on your experience and knowledge to consult, specify, and sell high\-end 3D printing equipment from a number of industry\-leading OEMs, including HP, Ultimaker, and AM Solutions, along with our 3D printing services. The commercial and enterprise sales territory is the upper East Coast. Meanwhile, the civilian government agencies, military, and Federal System Integrator (FSI) territories extend across the country.


This is a primarily outside, field\-based, sales role focusing on new business development ("hunter" mentality), strategic account penetration, and closing high\-value equipment opportunities. The role requires minimal in\-office presence, with the expectation of periodic visits (1–2x per month or as needed) to the Manassas, VA headquarters for internal alignment, training, and team collaboration. The ideal candidate is an experienced, disciplined, self\-starter salesperson who can operate independently in the field, while effectively leveraging internal resources, systems, and team support to close business.

**ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:**

* **Outside Sales**
* Complete prospecting of potential clients based on agreed markets and territory.
* Proactively identify and secure new client opportunities through cold calling, networking, and attending trade shows.
* Nurture and follow up with leads to generate meetings, both online and face to face, that move the client towards a purchase.
* Study, understand, and advocate for changes to technical and business requirements to make it both favorable for NCS and beneficial for clients.
* If a sale to a government entity requires an underlying contract vehicle, consult with NCS colleagues and provide the client with proper advice.
* Develop and maintain relationships with relevant sales organization and personnel of strategic OEM partners and leverage such relationships to acquire leads and preferential pricing.
* In\-person visits to potential and current clients.
* In\-depth follow\-up on leads provided by Marketing, OEMs, and other sources, including initial contact made by inside sales people.
* Maintain accurate, up\-to\-date records of all sales leads, results, and forecasts using current and future CRM protocols.
* Provide accurate quotes, literature, and application details to sales and clients as requested.
* Participate in all activities of the sales process that involve clients, outside sales, and other departments.
* Be a source of up to date product knowledge regarding current offerings including hardware and materials.
* **Internal Support**
* Maintain data regarding quotes, price list, CRM, and OEM literature.
* Collaborate with cross\-functional teams\-including Purchasing, Marketing, and Logistics\-to resolve client issues related to orders, shipments, installations, and ongoing service.
* Support Service and Demo Room production with client communications and appropriate escalation.
* Provide input and advice to Marketing to improve outcomes of their efforts.
* Develop and draft innovative ideas for incorporation by the Proposal Development team into NCS proposals.
* Coordinate initial Government paperwork and procurement handling for processing.
* Adhere to internal NCS procedures for order processing.
* Support Marketing efforts involving campaigns and tradeshows, with occasional overnight travel.

**QUALIFICATIONS:**

* Minimum 5\-10 years sales experience involving large equipment ($100k\-$500k deals).
* Experience and familiarity with industrial grade additive manufacturing equipment and materials.
* "Hunter" mentality to identify, pursue, and close deals.
* Familiarity and experience with is desired. key accounts from the Defense industry.
* Excellent time management for arranging and attending in\-person and virtual client meetings, while maintaining and coordinating inside sales support and order processing.
* Strong work ethic and ability to independently multi\-task on a sustained basis, based on objectives.
* Mastery of the English language, both spoken and written.
* Working knowledge of FAR/DFARS and Government/DOD procurement practices is desired.
* Proven ability to navigate the federal contracting lifecycle, from RFP response to contract execution.
* Overall understanding of 3D Print technologies and familiarity with the industry in general including its leading OEMs, distributors, service providers, and products and services.
* Ability to adhere to a Work in Office (WIO)/Work from Home (WFH) schedule as needed.
* Must be able to maintain a suitable working environment at place of residence to project a professional image in on\-line meetings with other parties.
* Willing and able to travel frequently by car, commercial airlines, and other means when appropriate.
* Strong interpersonal networking skills and able to build relationships with individuals with diverse personalities.
* Former military service in programs or organizations that can potentially make use of AM technologies is desired.
