Regional VP of Business Development

WeMake, Corp. · Remote, US Remote VP
Salary Range: $108K – $288K
Equity

**Overview**
We are a high\-growth cognitive well\-being service technology company seeking entrepreneurial operator/owners to join our fast\-growing cognitive screening and care delivery business. In this pivotal role, you will be taking charge of the screening service to detect cognitive impairment or dementia among seniors 50\+ in your designated area.

**This venture is a fast\-growing and very fulfilling business with great financial returns that offers a meaningful income and sustainable career/ business security in today's AI\-dominated economy. While bringing you great financial return, it also helps you make a great positive impact on people's lives in your community.**

**Responsibilities**

* Develop high\-quality target institutional clients in the target industry and designated region.
* Develop and execute comprehensive local\-oriented outreach and business development strategies within your assigned region to meet or exceed revenue targets.
* Build and nurture long\-term relationships with clients, understanding their unique challenges and delivering standardized solutions.
* Manage existing clients proactively by providing ongoing support, upselling services, and ensuring high levels of client satisfaction.
* Collaborate closely with headquarter marketing teams to develop targeted campaigns that generate leads and enhance brand visibility in your region.

**Requirements**

* Proven experience in B2B business operations, preferably within **senior care, high\-end wellness, golf clubs, cosmetic surgery, regenerative therapy, hospice, cognitive care, dementia care, MCI prevention, and residential building management** sectors, demonstrating successful business development outcomes.
* Strong service delivery and institutional client support skills with the ability to close deals effectively while maintaining positive client relationships.
* Excellent communication skills—both verbal and written—that facilitate clear presentations and persuasive discussions.
* Experience in technical sales or marketing is a plus, along with knowledge of the technology industry landscape.
