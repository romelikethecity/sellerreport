Account Executive, NetSuite

Pine Services Group · Remote, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $100K – $240K
MeddicChannelBuild TeamEquityUncappedOte Mentioned
Tools: SalesloftLinkedin Sales Navigator

**One of Pine's portfolio companies is hiring!**


**Job Title:** Account Executive, NetSuite


**Company**: Strategic Information Group


**Team/Department:** Sales


**Location:** Remote (U.S.)


**Compensation:** $100,000 \- $120,000 base \\u007C $200,000 \- $240,000 OTE

**Who We Are**

Strategic Information Group is an Oracle NetSuite Solution Provider. We don't just implement NetSuite \- we sell it. We are the single point of contact for our clients across the entire journey: license, implementation, training, and long\-term support. That full\-ownership model is a competitive advantage, and it makes our AEs more powerful closers.

We're growing fast and looking for a true hunter to help expand our customer base.

**The Role**

This is a pure net new logo role. No account management. No renewals. You own the full sales cycle \- from cold outreach to signed contract \- targeting organizations that have never worked with SIG before. You'll sell the complete package: NetSuite software licenses plus implementation and support services, giving you larger deal values and a single contract that closes everything at once. You'll engage CFOs, COOs, and operations leaders across manufacturing, wholesale distribution, and professional services.

**What You'll Do**


Build your own pipeline through outbound prospecting * \- calls, email sequences, LinkedIn, events, and referrals
* Run the full sales cycle: discovery, demo support, business case development, negotiation, and close
* Engage C\-suite decision\-makers with insight\-driven conversations tied to their specific business challenges
* Maintain 3\-4x pipeline coverage through disciplined, consistent prospecting activity
* Collaborate with solution consultants and the implementation team to deliver a seamless buying experience

Keep CRM updated with activity, pipeline stages, and forecasts * \- accurately and weekly
* Represent SIG at NetSuite events, user groups, and industry conferences
* Hit and exceed monthly and quarterly new logo quotas


**What We're Looking For**

Required:


* 2\-5 years of B2B SaaS or ERP sales experience (AE level) OR 5\+ years (Senior AE level)
* Proven track record hitting or exceeding quota on net new business
* Experience managing complex, multi\-stakeholder sales cycles (90\-180 days)

Strong outbound prospecting habits * \- you don't wait for inbound
* Excellent communication and presentation skills; comfortable presenting to C\-suite
* Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, Marketing, or related field (or equivalent experience)


Preferred:


* Hands\-on experience selling or using NetSuite (ERP, CRM, or financials modules)
* NetSuite certifications (SuiteFoundation, ERP Consultant, Administrator)
* Familiarity with MEDDIC, Challenger, or other structured sales methodologies
* Experience with outbound tools: Outreach, Salesloft, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, or similar
* Knowledge of manufacturing, wholesale distribution, or professional services verticals
* Prior experience at a NetSuite Solution Provider, VAR, or reseller


**What We Offer**


* $100,000 \- $120,000 base salary \\u007C $200,000 \- $240,000 OTE with uncapped commission and accelerators
* 100% remote

Sell licenses AND services as an authorized Solution Provider * \- larger deal values, cleaner closes

One contract, one relationship * \- no splitting deals with Oracle direct
* Direct support from solution consultants and technical pre\-sales resources
* Ongoing NetSuite training and Oracle partner enablement resources
* High\-accountability culture where performance is recognized and rewarded


**Travel**

Up to 20\-25% for prospect meetings, NetSuite events, user groups, and industry conferences.
