Account Executive - Debt Specialist (Remote)

Liberty Debt Solutions · Remote, US Remote Entry
Salary Range: $150K – $250K
Growth HireOte Mentioned

**Position Overview:**
We are looking for an enthusiastic and goal\-driven **Debt Relief Sales Specialist** to join our remote team. This role involves a high volume of outbound client engagement, following up on information requests and ensuring a seamless and supportive experience for our clients. This position offers unmatched earning potential with additional rewards through quarterly and annual performance bonuses **OTE $85K\-250K\+**

\*\*1099 Contract \- This is not a W\-2 Position\*\*

**Key Responsibilities:**

* Make outbound calls daily to follow up with clients, provide updates, and guide them through the debt relief process.
* Build strong relationships with clients by addressing their concerns and ensuring clarity at every step of the journey.
* Use CRM tools to manage client records, track interactions, and ensure timely follow\-ups.
* Collaborate with team members to provide exceptional client experiences and ensure a smooth process.
* Meet and exceed performance goals, including client engagement and satisfaction metrics (Metrics are heavily tracked).
* Stay organized and proactive, ensuring every client interaction is handled professionally and effectively.

**What We Offer:**

* A **fully remote** work environment with flexibility to manage your schedule.
* **Unmatched earning potential**, with additional rewards through quarterly and annual performance bonuses.
* Comprehensive training and ongoing support to help you excel in the role.
* A positive, team\-oriented culture with **opportunities for growth and advancement.**
* Access to state\-of\-the\-art tools and resources to maximize your productivity and efficiency.

**Requirements:**

* **Must have experience with Forth/Debt Pay Pro**
* **Must Debt Relief Sales Experience \- Top Performers Only**
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to build rapport quickly.
* Proven ability to handle a high volume of calls daily in a professional and effective manner.
* Highly organized and detail\-oriented, with strong time management skills.
* Comfortable using CRM tools to manage client interactions.
* Self\-motivated, results\-driven, and eager to succeed in a performance\-based environment.
* Prior experience in customer service, sales, or client success

Job Type: Contract
