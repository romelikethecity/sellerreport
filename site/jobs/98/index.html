Account Executive - Enterprise Sales

brightfin · Remote, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $125K – $250K
Solution SellingEnterpriseChannelOutboundEnterprise DealLongGrowth Hire
Tools: SalesforceSalesloftLinkedin Sales Navigator

**Company Overview:** brightfin provides clients with a suite of IT financial management and managed software subscriptions to enable your organization to rationalize, comprehend, normalize, optimize, and allocate spend to clearly show IT’s value in delivering revenue, services, and organizational enablement for the enterprise.

**Job Summary**: An Enterprise Account Executive is a key player in the sales team of a company, primarily focused on engaging with large\-scale corporate clients. They are responsible for driving sales and revenue by forging strong relationships with enterprise\-level customers.

**Essential Functions / Duties / Responsibilities**


* Understand brightfin’s suite of solutions and the value that we provide to our customers
* Ensure high levels of prospect satisfaction by utilizing value\-based, solution sellingmethodologies
* Accountable for closing new, upsell, and cross\-sell business and building pipeline
* Develop new business via outbound sales channels by identifying and engaging with appropriatepersonas/people within the target market
* Prepare and deliver compelling presentations and proposals on proposed solutions for yourprospect
* Maintain CRM hygiene
* Forecast your line of business for the next two quarters
* “Go the extra mile” to drive sales
* Build productive, trust\-based relationships with prospects
* Frequent knowledge sharing with teammates to ensure we are always learning


**Minimum Qualifications**


* 7\+ years of B2B SaaS sales experience, including 5\+ years selling enterprise\-level solutions with complex, multi‑stakeholder buying cycles.
* Proven track record of exceeding quotas, closing large enterprise deals, and driving new ARR growth.


**Required Knowledge, Skills \& Abilities**


* Experience leveraging value\-based, solution selling methodologies
* Demonstrated over\-achievement of sales goals and quotas
* Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills
* Experience collaborating with an active Partner Channel
* Proficient with: Microsoft Outlook, Microsoft Teams, Salesforce, SalesLoft/Outreach/equivalentand LinkedIn Sales Navigator
* Curious, entrepreneurial mindset
* Strong ability to work independently while contributing to a team environment
* Driven and competitive with a passion for winning


**Location:** US Based


**Compensation \& Benefits:**


* brightfin offers a comprehensive health, dental and vision benefits package.
* Paid time off. We strongly believe in work\-life balance and taking time for yourself.
* 401K with employer match
* Compensation Range: OTC: $125,000 \- $250,000
* Anticipated application window: May 1, 2026 \- May 8, 2026

FLSA Status: US Exempt

Physical Requirements: Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer.
