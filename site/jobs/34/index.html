Head of Business Development and Marketing

Tonkon Torp LLP · Portland, OR, US Remote Head of

Tonkon Torp LLP is seeking a strategic and experienced **Head of Business Development \& Marketing** to lead the firm’s business development and marketing function**.** Reporting to the COO and Managing Partner, this role drives firmwide business development efforts, strengthens market visibility, and ensures high\-quality marketing support to attorneys and firm leadership.

This position partners with attorneys and practice groups to develop and execute practical business development strategies, supports client and pitch efforts, and manages a three\-person team.

This is a highly visible, hands\-on leadership role for a professional who can build strong relationships, translate practice strengths into actionable plans, and bring consistency and discipline to growth efforts.

Since 1974, Tonkon Torp has provided exceptional legal counsel and business\-minded solutions to clients in Portland, Oregon and beyond. The firm’s legal support team plays a central role in helping attorneys deliver timely, high\-quality service.

**WHAT YOU'LL DO:**

* Lead and execute firmwide business development strategy across practice groups and attorneys
* Partner with attorneys to identify opportunities, build actionable plans, and strengthen client and referral relationships
* Support key pitches, proposals, and client development efforts
* Serve as a trusted advisor and coach to attorneys on business development and market visibility
* Drive marketing and brand initiatives, including events, rankings, and external profile\-raising activities
* Oversee development of marketing materials and ensure consistent, effective messaging
* Manage and develop a three\-person marketing team, including workflow, priorities, and service standards
* Improve processes, tools, and reporting to support effective execution and measurable results
* Monitor market trends and provide insight to guide strategy and investment

**REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:**

* Bachelor’s degree in marketing, business, or equivalent experience; law firm experience required
* 10\+ years of progressive business development and marketing experience, including leadership responsibility
* Proven success partnering with attorneys and practice leaders on business development strategy, client development, and execution (including planning, pitches, proposals, cross\-selling, and profile\-raising)
* Experience leading marketing initiatives that enhance brand visibility and market presence
* Demonstrated ability to lead, coach, and develop a team
* Strong executive presence, sound judgment, and ability to build credibility with attorneys and firm leadership
* Excellent communication, organizational, strategic thinking, and project management skills
* Ability to manage multiple priorities, meet deadlines, and perform in a fast\-paced environment with minimal supervision
* High attention to detail and proficiency with Microsoft Office, Adobe Acrobat (or similar), DocuSign, and other business systems

**PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:**

* Experience in a mid\-sized or large law firm with multiple practice groups
* Familiarity with legal directories, rankings, CRM tools, and pitch/proposal processes

**PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS AND WORKING CONDITIONS:**

The position involves sitting or standing at a desk for long periods, frequent computer use, clear communication with attorneys and staff, operation of standard office equipment, and work in a professional office environment with ability to work under tight deadlines and multitask.
