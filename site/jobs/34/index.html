Business Development Manager - EV and Clean Energy Solutions

McGrath · US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $188K – $210K
EnterpriseChannelEnterprise DealLongNorth AmericaGlobalFirst HireBuild TeamGrowth HireEquity
Tools: Salesforce

**Salary Range encompasses base \+ commission**

The specific pay rate and level, if applicable, will depend on the successful candidate’s qualifications, prior experience and location.* **Remote Position \- working from home with travel as needed based on project need.**

**“A Day in the Life”**
As a Business Development Manager, you will focus on business development in the Clean Energy Solutions industry focused on innovative, sustainable products and technologies for electric vehicle (EV) charging, off grid energy solutions, energy storage, energy security and outdoor media. You will be the lead on servicing existing client relationships and new client development to achieve overall growth objectives. You are responsible for developing strategies and tactics for identifying, pursuing and closing opportunities for selling EV and clean energy solutions to a variety of industry segments. This position requires new business generation, attention to detail, building customer relationships and working collaboratively across the organization. You will be working as a team with a Construction Manager that will support the project from Qualify to Contract and a Project Manager who will lead the project from Contract to Completion.

**“What You’ll Do”*** Proactively generate new leads and opportunities through cold\-calling, networking, and industry outreach.
* Qualify prospects and manage the sales funnel from initial contact to close.
* Develop customized proposals based on customer needs and company offerings.
* Educate prospective customers on the Company’s EV charging and Energy Solutions, and related products and services.
* Meet or exceed monthly and quarterly sales goals and KPIs.
* Track and manage all sales activity using CRM (Salesforce) tools.
* Build long\-term relationships with clients, business owners, facility managers, and commercial developers.
* Collaborate with internal teams (construction services, engineering, marketing, customer service, finance) to support deal flow and customer satisfaction.
* Represent the Company at trade shows, networking events, and local energy/EV related events.
* Strategic Sales Leadership: Develop and execute comprehensive sales strategies for growth across B2B, B2C, and government segments, aligning with business objectives and identifying new market opportunities.
* Marketing – Lead brand strategy, positioning, and storytelling across channels to drive awareness and demand. Oversee digital marketing, content strategy, events, and campaigns to support product launches and customer acquisition. Ensure consistency in brand voice across customer touchpoints (website, social media, trade shows and press).
* Market Analysis \& Expansion: Conduct market research and competitive analysis to identify trends and growth areas and develop strategies for market penetration and expansion.
* Client \& Partnership Management: Cultivate relationships with strategic customers (fleet buyers, commercial clients, municipalities, utilities) and partners, lead complex negotiations, and secure strategic alliances to expand reach and accelerate growth. Expand channel and distribution partnerships both domestically and internationally. Identify and close major enterprise and government sales opportunities.
* Perform other duties as assigned

**“Must Haves”*** 10\+ years of progressive sales leadership experience with a track record of success in the EV charging or Renewable Energy technology sectors.
* Proven success in cold calling and developing new business from the ground up.
* Self\-starter who thrives in a fast\-paced, mission\-driven environment.
* Bachelor’s degree in business administration, Marketing, or a related field. MBA preferred.
* Experience scaling revenue at a fast\-growing or public company.
* Proven ability to develop and execute sales strategies, exceed revenue targets and develop and manage sales teams.
* Strong understanding of the EV charging industry, Renewable Energy market dynamics, and competitive landscape.
* Expertise in B2B and potentially B2C sales models, including managing complex enterprise sales cycles.
* Experience with sales forecasting, pipeline management, and CRM software (e.g., Salesforce).
* Excellent leadership, communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills.
* Strong analytical and problem\-solving abilities, with a data\-driven approach.
* Ability to thrive in a fast\-paced environment and adapt to evolving market conditions.
* Strong written and oral communication skills
* Strong sales skills
* Strong interpersonal skills
* Strong computer skills, emphasis on Microsoft Office, Internet and other customer databases
* Experience selling EV\-related technology, Energy Solutions or solar (commercial or residential) is highly preferred.
* Must be able to travel via motor vehicle or airline to various customer locations
* Call on customer, face to face, via phone and email to establish relationships and market the company
* Valid Driver’s License

**“Perks”*** It’s a great place to work where you are given the space to share ideas and opinions and the bottom\-line is “YOU” matter!
* We value our employees' Quality of Life, and when it comes to mental health, recovery, and self\-care, there is no one\-size\-fits\-all approach that is why we provide resources where employees can find information on several wellness topics like nutrition, exercise, emotional health, and more!
* We all need to rest and recharge, that’s why in addition to vacation and sick time we also offer 10 days \+ 2 half days company\-paid holidays!
* We offer a variety of benefits that offer you flexibility and choice, a simple selection experience, and the ability to take control over your benefit spending.
* 401(k) Retirement Plan with Company Match.
* Life is unpredictable, having Company Paid Life Insurance and knowing that your loved ones will be protected financially in the event of an unexpected death can give you peace of mind.
* We also offer DailyPay, HSA, FSA, EAP and Pet Insurance!
