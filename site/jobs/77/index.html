Account Executive, Opticom Solutions

Miovision Technologies · CA, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $183K – $216K
Solution SellingEnterprise DealLongNorth AmericaGlobalGrowth HireEquity
Tools: Salesforce

**About Miovision:**

At Miovision, we’re unlocking transportation networks that move you. Our vision and mission is to enable smart, fast, safe communities that simply flow, as we drive the Intelligent Mobility Revolution. Backed by the world’s most advanced traffic AI, Miovision’s innovations in traffic signal planning and operations are making it possible for cities to improve the transportation experience for drivers, cyclists and pedestrians.

Our values drive us. They’re at the core of everything we do. If they align with yours, proceed through the GREEN light!

* **All in to win:** We're driven by a winning mindset, approaching every challenge with intensity, clarity, and speed.
* **One Miovision:** We succeed as one team, uniting diverse talents, building on trust, and putting our shared mission before ego.
* **Be better every day:** We're committed to continuous growth, staying curious, building mastery, and embracing challenges as learning opportunities.
* **Make it happen:** We are proactive and results\-driven, taking ownership, acting with urgency, and focusing on solutions that deliver real impact.
* **Earn the customer:** We are deeply customer\-centric, focused on earning our customers' partnership every day by delivering exceptional experiences that drive their success.

**Position Summary:**

Miovision’s Opticom product has been providing best\-in\-class priority control solutions for over 58 years. A testament to Opticom’s quality and durability is the continuing growth in the customer base, now at over 180,000 intersections and vehicles with over 5,000 customers worldwide. Our success is based on working with communities to provide the best emergency vehicle preemption and transit signal priority systems.


As an Account Executive for Opticom Solutions, you'll be the driver for new business across California's 1st Responder and Transit markets, while also maintaining momentum of our existing relationships to expand their accounts. Your mission is to ensure Miovision always gets the green light and help cities improve the safety, reliability, and efficiency of their emergency and transit vehicles, optimize traffic flow, reduce congestion and enhance smart mobility.

**Objectives and Responsibilities:**

* **Deliver Results:** Achieve or exceed quarterly and annual revenue targets by prioritizing high\-value prospects and key accounts within a defined territory.
* **Own the Sales Lifecycle:** Independently manage the end\-to\-end sales process, from discovery and needs analysis to contract negotiation and closing.
* **Build Pipeline:** Make prospecting part of your regular routine.
* **Strategic Forecasting and Growth:** Develop and execute territory plans that maintain a robust, multi\-stage pipeline of opportunities.
* **Product Champion:** Act as the region’s subject matter expert for Miovision Opticom products and services. Champion the transition from hardware\-based to cloud\-based EVP systems.
* **Trusted Advisor:** Become a trusted advisor to city departments to uncover their needs and articulate the value of Miovision's solutions.
* **Strategic Opportunity Management:** Lead the coordination and development of RFP and RFI responses to position Miovision as the preferred vendor for modern traffic solutions.
* **Maintain Data Integrity:** Use Salesforce with discipline to provide leadership with accurate forecasts and clear visibility into your pipeline.
* **Industry Trends:** Stay sharp on market challenges and the competitive landscape to effectively position Miovision’s value proposition.
* **Ecosystem Collaboration:** Work collaboratively with Partners, Business Development and Marketing teams to launch regional campaigns. Engage an array of external influencers, collaborate with and drive distribution partner sellers to be a force multiplier.
* **Brand Ambassador:** Represent Miovision in person on site with clients and at major trade shows. Travel up to 40%.

**Ideal Profile:**

* **Specialized Sales Knowledge:** Proven experience selling technology to the 1st Responder Market (specifically to Fire, EMS, and police). You understand consultative selling methodologies, and how to navigate long cycles and public procurement processes (RFPs).
* **Domain Expertise:** Understanding of the Transportation/Traffic or Public Safety sector.
* **Strategic Acumen:** You don't just wait for leads; you build territory plans and maintain a robust, multi\-stage pipeline of opportunities (Salesforce or similar CRM proficiency is a must).
* **Hybrid Technology Fluency:** Equally comfortable navigating the nuances of software systems and hardware\-integrated traffic solutions and you can effectively communicate the value of both.
* **"Hunter" Mindset:** You thrive on finding new business and have a proven track record of navigating multi\-dimensional, complex sales environments.
* **Influence and Negotiation:** Advanced negotiation and presentation skills, with the ability to influence and engage with a variety of personas ranging from Fire Chief to Signal Technician.
* **Self\-driven:** You are highly self\-motivated and comfortable operating autonomously in a fast\-paced, evolving industry.
* **The Networker:** You excel at creating external visibility and fostering internal collaboration.
* **Travel:** You’re ready to get out into the field, meet customers, and represent us at major trade shows (up to 40%). You must be able to travel internationally and move freely between the USA and Canada.

**This is a remote opportunity for candidates located in California.**

**Your Rewards \& Well\-being:**

We invest in our team with benefits designed for modern life and true work\-life balance.

* **Comprehensive Coverage:** Your well\-being is covered from day one with comprehensive health benefits, 24/7 virtual healthcare access, and dedicated wellness programs.
* **Financial Future:** Build for tomorrow with our RRSP/401K Matching Plan and share in the company's success through our Variable Incentive Plan.
* **Time to Recharge:** Truly unplug with our unique Mio\-Days and flexible vacation policy.
* **Work \& Life Support:** We support you with flexible work options, an internet subsidy, a remote work allowance, and enhanced leave for new parents.

**Sound like your next adventure? Apply now and let's start building together!**
