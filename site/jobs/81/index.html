Regional Sales Manager (Laboratory Testing) - Mid-Atlantic/Northeast

Ascend Clinical · Remote, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $175K – $225K
North AmericaGrowth HireTurnaroundEquityOte Mentioned

### **Regional Sales Manager (Laboratory Testing)**

### **Location: Remote (Mid\-Atlantic/Northeast)**

### **Why Ascend?**

### **Come work for a company that is transforming the industry!**


We are Ascend, one of the highest volume clinical laboratories in the United States. With the use of the most advanced tools and technology, we process millions of tests each month for our client base comprised of over 75 hospitals and over 2,300 dialysis clinics. For over 20 years, we have been delivering industry\-leading service and excellence in testing. At Ascend, we are relentless about innovation and growing to pioneer the future of clinical and environmental laboratory testing.

Ascend offers the discipline of a healthcare leader and the mentality of a tech startup. Along with our automated laboratory we have internally developed a primary customer\-facing platform that assists with managing laboratory services, quality assurance and performance improvement reporting. As a company that is on the leading edge, we are seeking individuals with a similar mindset who enjoy a dynamic, fast\-paced environment.

**Job Summary:**

This position is responsible for driving sales growth, building strong relationships with healthcare providers, and expanding our market presence within the assigned area of responsibility. The ideal candidate will have a deep understanding of selling laboratory testing services along with the ability to work independently while achieving ambitious targets. This is a remote role covering the Mid\-Atlantic and Northeast territory.

**Responsibilities:*** Responsible for selling laboratory services for dialysis/CKD, diagnostics, transplant/post\-transplant, and other future testing that Ascend will offer to hospitals, clinics, physician offices, and other potential partners within the United States and potentially abroad
* Establishes, maintains, and strengthens client relationships at the appropriate levels. Generates and delivers qualified opportunities through cold calling, lead generation, networking, and strategic partnerships
* Showcase Ascend Clinical’s capabilities to potential clients. Ability to differentiate Ascend Clinical from competitors
* Develops and maintains actionable account plans
* Creates and evaluates quotations for services and provides inputs to ensure client and company requirements are met
* Provide input of business plans on the market vision through desk research (legislation, market demand, trends and developments, competitor analysis)
* Maintain face\-to\-face contact with customers and develop multi\-level and cross functional relationships with key customers
* Responsible for opportunity management and accurate pipeline forecasting
* Collaborates effectively with Ascend leadership to bring potential opportunities to their attention and to identify and win projects. Provides cross\-functional support to other departments as required
* Owns the accountability and responsibility of delivering to client needs and timeliness

**Qualifications:*** Understanding and knowledge of laboratory testing and ability to explain and sell to prospective clients. Ability to clearly engage on benefits of Ascend’s laboratory fit. 5\+ years sales experience required
* Bonus if have experience with dialysis/CKD, diagnostics, or transplant
* Strategic thinker with a proactive approach to identifying and pursuing new business opportunities
* Ability to travel 30% of the time for client meetings, trade shows, industry meetings, and events
* Competent computer skills, including MS Office
* Excellent written and verbal communication skills
* Demonstrated effective negotiation skills
* Detail oriented, critical and analytical thinking skills, and problem\-solving skills

**Compensation:**

* $175,000\-$225,000 OTE

**Benefits:**

**As an Ascend Clinical employee, you will become part of a company that has received national recognition as a great place to work. We offer excellent full\-time benefits including comprehensive medical coverage, life and disability insurance, 401(k) with company match, paid holidays and vacation, personal days, and dental and vision options.**
