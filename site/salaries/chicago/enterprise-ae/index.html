Enterprise Account Executive Salary in Chicago 2026

By Rome Thorndike · 2026-05-15 · 471 words

Enterprise Account Executive roles in Chicago pay a median base salary of $132K in 2026, with on-target earnings (OTE) at approximately $220K for reps at full quota attainment. The full range spans $108K to $161K on base salary depending on company stage, segment, and rep experience.

Enterprise Account Executive in Chicago at a glance: Median base $132K. Median OTE $220K. Typical split: 60/40 base/variable. Quota: $1.2M-$2.5M annual ARR.

How Chicago compares to the national median

The US national median for Enterprise Account Executive roles is $120K. Chicago pay runs 10% above the national figure. Chicago combines enterprise SaaS, financial services, and B2B services sales. Compensation runs near the US median but cost of living below SF, NYC, and Boston produces strong real-income outcomes.

Chicago has a cost-of-living index of 107 versus the US average of 100. After cost-of-living adjustment, a $132K Enterprise Account Executive salary in Chicago converts to approximately $123K in national-equivalent purchasing power. Role context: Enterprise Account Executive

Enterprise AEs run six-figure-and-up deals across multi-stakeholder evaluations. Cycle lengths run 6-12 months. Compensation skews higher with stronger accelerators. In Chicago, Enterprise Account Executive compensation typically follows a 60/40 base/variable base-to-variable split with quota expectations of $1.2M-$2.5M annual ARR. Strong performers earn 110-150% of OTE through accelerators above plan.

What drives the high and low ends

The high end of the Chicago Enterprise Account Executive range ($161K base) is concentrated at enterprise SaaS, financial services, and infrastructure vendors with strong product-market fit. The low end ($108K base) shows up at early-stage startups, SMB-focused vendors, and roles with heavier variable compensation built into the OTE structure.

Equity matters more in Chicago than in lower-cost metros. Roughly 60-75% of senior Enterprise Account Executive roles in Chicago include equity grants. Public-company RSU packages typically run $20K-$80K per year for AE roles and higher for management roles.
