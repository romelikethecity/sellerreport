Account Executive Salary in Denver 2026: Base, OTE, Quota

By Rome Thorndike · 2026-05-15 · 470 words

Account Executive roles in Denver pay a median base salary of $76K in 2026, with on-target earnings (OTE) at approximately $152K for reps at full quota attainment. The full range spans $62K to $92K on base salary depending on company stage, segment, and rep experience.

Account Executive in Denver at a glance: Median base $76K. Median OTE $152K. Typical split: 50/50 base/variable. Quota: $600K-$1.2M annual ARR.

How Denver compares to the national median

The US national median for Account Executive roles is $90K. Denver pay runs 16% below the national figure. Denver's sales market grew through the 2020-2024 SaaS relocations and remains a top mid-tier US metro for sales hiring. Lower cost of living than SF or Boston stretches take-home pay further.

Cost-of-living adjustment

Denver has a cost-of-living index of 121 versus the US average of 100. After cost-of-living adjustment, a $76K Account Executive salary in Denver converts to approximately $62K in national-equivalent purchasing power. Sales professionals weighing offers across cities should compare cost-adjusted pay rather than nominal salary.

Role context: Account Executive

Account Executives carry quota for closing new-business deals. The largest single role in B2B sales hiring, ranging from SMB AEs through Enterprise AEs. In Denver, Account Executive compensation typically follows a 50/50 base/variable base-to-variable split with quota expectations of $600K-$1.2M annual ARR. Strong performers earn 110-150% of OTE through accelerators above plan.

What drives the high and low ends

The high end of the Denver Account Executive range ($92K base) is concentrated at enterprise SaaS, financial services, and infrastructure vendors with strong product-market fit. The low end ($62K base) shows up at early-stage startups, SMB-focused vendors, and roles with heavier variable compensation built into the OTE structure.

Equity matters more in Denver than in lower-cost metros. Roughly 60-75% of senior Account Executive roles in Denver include equity grants. Public-company RSU packages typically run $20K-$80K per year for AE roles and higher for management roles.
