Vice President of Business Development

AlohaCare · Remote, US Remote VP
Salary Range: $180K – $200K

**The Company:**

AlohaCare is a local, non-profit health plan serving Hawai`i’s low-income residents with free Medicaid and dual Medicare health insurance coverage. Our members include children, seniors and adults residing on all islands. We provide comprehensive benefits and managed care services with an emphasis on healthy living habits and preventive primary health care. Our approach is to meet the whole-person health and social needs of members. Through our community partnerships we offer innovative services such as connection to social service agencies, Native Hawaiian healing services and in-home primary care for qualified members. Our mission is to serve in the true spirit of aloha by ensuring and advocating for access to quality health care for all. We are Hawaii’s third-largest health plan and offer a wide network of quality primary care, specialists, hospitals, pharmacies and among many other providers across the state.

**The Culture:**

AlohaCare employees have a passion for helping Hawai`i’s most underserved communities. Our caring culture is fundamental to our company-wide team approach to providing high quality services. We support our employees with a supportive and positive work environment, healthy work-life balance, continuous communication, and a generous benefits package.

AlohaCare’s leadership empowers and engages its employees by recognizing outstanding job performance and collaboration. We share organization-wide updates during quarterly All Staff meetings. We encourage participation in volunteer and educational opportunities. We put a high value on honesty, respect, and trust-building. We encourage open-door, two-way, and frequent communication.

AlohaCare’s comprehensive benefits package includes low-cost medical, dental, drug and vision insurance, paid time-off, 401k employer contribution, referral bonus and pretax transportation and parking program.

**The Opportunity:**

The Vice President of Business Development is a senior leadership role responsible for driving AlohaCare’s strategic growth initiatives, expanding Medicaid and Medicare Advantage membership, and strengthening our position as Hawaii’s leading locally managed health plan. Reporting directly to the Chief Executive Officer, the VP will lead a dynamic team in identifying new business opportunities, cultivating relationships with potential clients, community organizations, providers, and advocacy groups, and developing strategies that support the company’s long-term vision and mission to serve Hawaii’s underserved communities. In addition, the Vice President is accountable for the financial performance of products, developing and implementing financial risk mitigation strategies—including cost control, diversification of funding sources, and proactive engagement with state and federal agencies—and researching, proposing, and implementing innovative new products and services to ensure sustained growth. This position is hybrid, offering a combination of remote and in-office work, and requires travel as often as monthly to AlohaCare’s offices.

**Primary Duties and Responsibilities:**

* Own the P&L with an entrepreneurial mindset of the overall strategy and operations of the MSO including the funds flow between all related parties.
* Develop and execute comprehensive business development strategies aligned with AlohaCare’s organizational goals, with an immediate focus on Medicaid and Medicare Advantage growth and expansion to commercial in later phases
* Identify and pursue new markets, clients, and partnership opportunities to drive revenue growth and strengthen AlohaCare’s position in Hawaii’s healthcare market.
* Ensure strong alignment with AlohaCare managed care (Health Plan) entity to establish a framework that creates flywheel opportunities for both related entities. Opportunities include but are not limited to finance, clinical, network, product and community partnerships
* Lead negotiations and manage relationships with strategic partners, clients, community organizations, providers, advocacy groups, and stakeholders.
* Cultivate partnerships to advance health equity and culturally responsive care for Hawaii’s underserved populations.
* Responsible for the financial performance of all products within the business portfolio, including developing and implementing financial risk mitigation strategies, cost control, and funding diversification.
* Research, propose, and implement new products and services that support the company’s growth, innovation, and operational efficiency objectives.
* Drive operational efficiency and process improvement initiatives to optimize cost structure and enhance service delivery.
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including Marketing, Sales, Product, Operations, and Compliance, to ensure seamless execution of business initiatives.
* Oversee market research, competitive analysis, and data analytics to inform business decisions, measure impact, and refine strategy.
* Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and leverage data analytics and performance metrics to report regularly on progress to executive leadership and drive continuous improvement.
* Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations and lead advocacy efforts to influence policy and funding decisions at the state and federal levels.
* Represent the company at industry conferences, events, and meetings to enhance brand presence, network, and advocate for AlohaCare’s mission.
* Mentor and develop the business development team, fostering a culture of collaboration, high performance, emotional intelligence, adaptability, and change leadership.
* Develop strategies to enhance member engagement, satisfaction, and retention, with a focus on vulnerable populations.
* Manage key stakeholder engagement – Board, clients, partners, regulator and others as needed to execute high performing governance model without creating unnecessary overhead

* All other duties assigned.
* Adhere to regulatory compliance and quality guidelines as well as AlohaCare policies and procedures.
* Responsible for maintaining AlohaCare’s confidential information in accordance with AlohaCare policies, and state and federal laws, rules and regulations regarding confidentiality. Employees have access to AlohaCare data based on the data classification assigned to this job title.
