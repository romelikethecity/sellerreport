At RTX, the world largest aerospace and defense company, 185,000 great minds are united by purpose and inspired to make a difference solving the world’s most complex problems. Collins Aerospace is a leader in technologically advanced, intelligent solutions that help redefine the aerospace and defense industry. With a comprehensive portfolio and deep technical expertise, we help customers meet the demands of the global market. Join us and help shape the future of aerospace and defense.


This position is for a senior business development professional that will be dedicated to the airborne and strategic HF communications market within our Missions Systems/Resilient Communications Products business. The environment is fast\-paced, technical and challenging. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in US Do W and airborne communication systems requirements. Particularly a background in airborne HF communications systems in the US Air Force and US Navy is desirable. Candidates should have a thorough understanding of the US DoW requirements, budget and procurement processes, and be comfortable interacting with DoW subject matter experts and mid\-tier leadership in all areas of the acquisition process. Experience with organizations such as USAF A5/A6/A8, USN N2N6/N98, USAF AFNWC, USAF ACC, USAF ACC, AFGSC, USSTRATCOM NEC, NAVWAR, PMA 209 and PEO IWS is desired.

**This position can work remotely; however, it will be ideal to be located near one of the following locations: Cedar Rapids, IA, Marlborough, MA, or Richardson, TX.**

**What You Will Do**

* The successful candidate will work in conjunction with program management and engineering leads, to uncover, pursue and capture new business in the military airborne and strategic HF communication market
* Responsible for developing, forecasting, and capturing new opportunities and maintaining relationships with existing customers
* Develops and presents compelling strategic and business growth opportunities that may include market and competitive analysis via decision point reviews
* Continually scans environment for business and strategic growth opportunities
* Leading and working cross\-functional, cross\-enterprise teams
* Prepares and supports company profiles on competitors and potential acquisition candidates, including financial analyses
* Networks with senior internal and external personnel in area of expertise. Represents organization as a prime contact on projects. Interacts with senior internal and external personnel on significant matters, often requiring coordination between organizations
* Works on problems where analysis of situations or data requires a review of multiple factors. Demonstrates good judgment in selecting methods and techniques for obtaining solutions, where erroneous decisions or failure to achieve objectives would have a serious impact on the organization
* Normally receives minimal supervision on day\-to\-day work and general instructions on new assignments
* Travel expectation is 25\-30%

**Qualifications You Must Have**

* This position requires a Bachelor’s degree in the appropriate discipline and 10 years of relevant experience or an Advanced degree in the appropriate discipline and 7 years of relevant experience. In the absence of a degree, 14 years of relevant experience is required.
* **Active** and **transferable** U.S. government issued **Secret** Security Clearance is required prior to start date. U.S. citizenship is required, as only U.S. citizens are eligible for a security clearance
* Minimum of 10 years’ experience through a combination of military, government and/or defense electronic industry positions
* Experience with military communication systems, capabilities, and technologies
* Experience with Microsoft Office products

**Qualifications We Prefer**

* Experience with SalesForce CRM tool is a plus but not required
* Background in Airborne HF communications systems in the US Air Force and US Navy is highly desirable
