Inside Sales Account Manager

DIVERSE SUPPLY INC · Remote, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $60K – $200K
InsideInboundGlobalGrowth HireEquityUncapped

**Overview**

At **Diverse Supply, Inc.,** we are a leading internet marketing firm representing manufacturers and suppliers of industrial maintenance, access and related material handling equipment. We provide businesses nationally and internationally with high\-quality solutions for warehousing, manufacturing, aviation, and facility maintenance applications. Through our website, **www.platformsandladders.com,** we deliver specialized equipment backed by exceptional service, technical expertise, and industry knowledge.

We are seeking a dynamic and results\-driven **Inside** **Sales Account Manager** to join our energetic sales team. In this pivotal role, you will be responsible for cultivating strong relationships with clients, identifying new business opportunities, and driving revenue growth through strategic account management. Your enthusiasm and proactive approach will empower you to engage with potential customers, understand their needs, and deliver tailored solutions that foster long\-term partnerships. If you thrive in a fast\-paced environment and are passionate about industrial equipment sales and business development, this is your chance to make a significant impact!

**Duties**

* Manage inbound sales inquiries and provide technical sales support by explaining product features, benefits, and integrations to customers with varying levels of technical knowledge
* Coordinate with manufacturers/vendors on pricing, specs, and lead times
* Quote products and freight, aggressively follow up on bids/opportunities
* Oversee orders from quote to delivery
* Build long\-term B2B customer relationships and grow accounts
* Engage with existing clients to expand account relationships, identify upselling opportunities, and ensure customer satisfaction.
* Collaborate with marketing teams to execute targeted campaigns and generate new leads for business development.
* Utilize CRM tools to track interactions, update account information, and analyze sales data for strategic planning.
* Maintain organized records in QuickBooks and internal systems.
* Develop and maintain a robust pipeline of B2B sales opportunities through warm calling, email outreach, and networking

**Requirements**

* Proven experience in industrial equipment sales with a track record of meeting or exceeding targets.
* 3\+ years of B2B industrial sales experience
* Strong negotiation skills coupled with the ability to build rapport quickly through warm calling and effective communication.
* Demonstrated expertise in business development strategies that drive revenue growth.
* Strong understanding of industrial equipment/product sales
* Excellent communication skills with the ability to articulate complex technical solutions clearly and persuasively.
* Analytical mindset capable of assessing market trends, customer needs, and competitive landscapes to inform sales strategies.
* QuickBooks proficiency preferred
* Ability to work independently in a remote environment
* Leadership qualities that inspire teamwork, motivate colleagues, and foster a positive sales environment.

**Compensation**

We value hard work and reward results. Our comp plans are competitive and inspire success:

**Uncapped Monthly Commissions with Tiered Monthly Sales Bonuses**

**1st Year avg $60k with 2nd year reps averaging $80k\+. Top earners make 200k\+**

Join us as an Inside Sales Account Manager where your energy fuels our success! Bring your passion for industrial equipment/product sales, sharpen your negotiation skills, and help us shape the future of innovative solutions for our customers worldwide!

Work Location: Remote
