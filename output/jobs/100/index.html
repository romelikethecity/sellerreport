Sr. Sales Manager

Company not listed · Santa Clara, CA, US Remote Mid
Salary Range: $150K – $250K
Solution SellingEnterpriseMid DealLongGlobalGrowth HireEquity
Tools: Salesforce

**Location:**US Based (Remote) Santa Clara, CA Preferred
**Type:** Full-Time
**Department:** Sales & Business Development
**Reports To:** Head of Sales

### **Why Join Auradine?**

Auradine delivers next-generation infrastructure for blockchain, security, and decentralized computing—spanning ASICs, immersion-cooled systems, and full-stack platforms. Backed by tier-1 investors and led by industry veterans, we are solving hard problems that power critical workloads at global scale. This is a unique opportunity to join a category-defining company and play a pivotal role in driving enterprise growth and strategic account execution.

### **Position Overview**

We are seeking a high-performing **Sr. Sales Manager** to lead complex enterprise sales and business development engagements, drive revenue growth, and help shape Auradine’s go-to market strategy. This role is ideal for a senior sales professional with deep experience in solution selling, enterprise relationship development, and global account management—ideally in infrastructure technology or hardware-based systems.

You will own strategic opportunities from origination to close, while collaborating cross functionally to align customer success with Auradine’s product and operational roadmap.

### **Key Responsibilities:**

* Develop and execute a targeted sales strategy to expand enterprise footprint and accelerate revenue
* Manage high-value global accounts across the blockchain and infrastructure ecosystem, including data center operators, mining firms, and energy-aligned partners
* Drive large, complex solution sales cycles from ASIC-level to system-level deployments
* Build and deepen relationships with C-level decision makers and technical stakeholders
* Align with internal product, engineering, marketing, and operations teams to deliver customer-specific solutions and support execution at scale
* Represent Auradine at industry forums, strategic partner summits, and customer events
* Lead contract negotiations, pricing strategy, and deal structuring for long-term customer value
* Maintain detailed sales reporting, forecasts, and pipeline visibility through Salesforce and internal tools
* Serve as a customer advocate and escalation point, ensuring alignment and satisfaction throughout the lifecycle

### **What You Bring:**

* 10+ years of enterprise sales experience in the technology sector, selling to enterprise accounts
* Proven track record selling complex infrastructure solutions—ASICs, systems, or integrated hardware/software platforms
* Demonstrated success managing multi-million-dollar sales cycles and strategic accounts
* Strong strategic mindset with ability to balance long-term growth and quarterly execution
* Excellent interpersonal, communication, and executive engagement skills
* Deep familiarity with Salesforce and CRM-driven pipeline discipline
* Ability to travel up to 50%

### **Preferred Qualifications:**

* Experience selling into blockchain, infrastructure, or data center markets
* Background working with mining systems, sovereign or energy-led initiatives
* Entrepreneurial mindset with experience in early-stage or high-growth environments

### **Compensation**

* Base Salary Range: $150,000 – $250,000
* Variable Compensation: Performance-based incentive plan
* Equity: Stock options aligned to role and experience
* Benefits: Comprehensive healthcare, 401(k), PTO, and more
