Usage Notes

  • Minimum size: Don't use the logo smaller than 100px wide (horizontal) or 80px (icon only)
  • Clear space: Maintain padding equal to the icon height around the logo
  • Favicon: For ICO files, convert the SVG to PNG at 16px and 32px, then combine into ICO format
  • Social profiles: Platforms will crop to circle — the icon-only version works best
  • Font: System font stack (-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Segoe UI, Roboto)
