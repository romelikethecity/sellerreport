Best smooth.AI Alternatives in 2026

By Rome Thorndike · 2026-04-02 · 7 tools reviewed

smooth.AI promises unlimited contacts powered by AI. The reality: data accuracy is inconsistent, the UI pushes aggressive upsells, and "unlimited" comes with asterisks. If you've tried smooth and found the data quality lacking, these alternatives deliver more reliable results.

Top Pick
Apollo.io 8.6/10
Starting at Free / $49/mo Best for: SDRs and startups who need data + outreach in one tool

Apollo is the most natural switch from smooth.AI. You get a bigger database, better data quality, built-in sequencing, and a free tier that's generous. The UI is more polished, pricing is transparent, and you won't get ambushed by upsells every time you log in.

PROS
  • Massive database with generous free tier
  • Built-in email sequencing and dialer
  • Exceptional value vs. competitors
CONS
  • Email accuracy lower than ZoomInfo for enterprise contacts
  • UI can feel overwhelming with so many features
  • Phone numbers less reliable than dedicated providers
Verum 8.5/10
Starting at $2,000 Best for: Midmarket sales teams (5K+ records) that need clean, enriched data without managing another platform

Skip the self-serve grind. Send your target criteria, get back enriched contacts from 50+ sources with human QA. Different model, often better results for batch prospecting.

PROS
  • 50+ data sources with human QA on every record
  • 93% email deliverability guarantee
  • Full-service: cleaning, enrichment, and validation in one engagement
CONS
  • Not a platform. No login, no dashboard, no real-time API
  • Minimum $2,000 project size isn't ideal for very small teams
  • Turnaround is 24-48 hours, not instant
ZoomInfo 8.5/10
Starting at $14,995/yr Best for: Mid-market and enterprise sales teams with budget for premium data

The gold standard for enterprise B2B data. Massive database, strong intent signals. But the pricing starts at $15K/yr and climbs fast.

PROS
  • Largest B2B contact database (260M+ profiles)
  • Built-in intent data and workflow automation
  • Strong Salesforce and HubSpot integrations
CONS
  • Expensive. Minimum $15K/year with annual contracts
  • Data accuracy varies by segment and industry
  • Auto-renewal contracts catch people off guard
Lusha 7.5/10
Starting at Free / $29/mo Best for: Individual reps and small teams needing quick contact lookups

Lightweight prospecting tool with solid direct dial data. Good for individual reps, but lacks the depth of full-platform solutions.

PROS
  • Simple and fast Chrome extension
  • Good direct dial accuracy
  • Affordable entry point for small teams
CONS
  • Smaller database than Apollo or ZoomInfo
  • Limited workflow automation
  • Credit-based model gets expensive at scale
Cognism 7.8/10
Starting at Custom pricing Best for: Teams selling into EMEA markets who need GDPR-compliant data

European-first B2B data with strong GDPR compliance. Diamond Data phone-verified contacts work well for outbound calling into EMEA.

PROS
  • Best EMEA data coverage in the market
  • Phone-verified Diamond Data for direct dials
  • GDPR and CCPA compliant by design
CONS
  • Weaker North American coverage than ZoomInfo or Apollo
  • No free tier and pricing isn't transparent
  • US-focused teams often find better options elsewhere
UpLead 7.1/10
Starting at $99/mo Best for: Small teams and startups that need affordable B2B data

Budget B2B data provider with decent accuracy for the price. 95% accuracy guarantee sounds good on paper, but coverage gaps show up in niche verticals.

PROS
  • 95% data accuracy guarantee with credit refunds
  • Real-time email verification on export
  • Straightforward pricing with no hidden fees
CONS
  • Smaller database than Apollo or ZoomInfo
  • Limited phone number coverage
  • Feature set is basic compared to full-platform tools
Lead411 7.2/10
Starting at $99/mo Best for: Mid-market teams that want intent data bundled with contacts

Mid-tier data provider with built-in intent data (Bombora-powered). Decent value for teams that want both contacts and buying signals without paying ZoomInfo prices.

PROS
  • Intent data included at every pricing tier
  • Unlimited email exports on higher plans
  • Good value for the feature set
CONS
  • Database is smaller than the big three (ZoomInfo, Apollo, Cognism)
  • UI feels dated compared to newer tools
  • Phone data accuracy could be better

Frequently Asked Questions

Is smooth.AI's data unlimited?

Not really. Higher-tier plans advertise unlimited credits, but there are daily and monthly search caps that aren't always disclosed upfront. You'll also find that "unlimited" doesn't mean "accurate." Many contacts returned by smooth have outdated emails or wrong phone numbers, so the raw volume doesn't translate to usable pipeline.

Why do people switch away from smooth.AI?

The three most common complaints are data accuracy, aggressive upselling, and contract lock-in. Reps report bounce rates north of 30% on email exports, and the sales team pushes hard for annual commitments. Once you're locked in, cancellation isn't straightforward. Most teams that leave end up at Apollo or ZoomInfo.

What's the cheapest good alternative to smooth.AI?

Apollo's free tier gives you 10,000 email credits per month, which is more than most reps need. If you need phone numbers, UpLead starts at $74/mo with a 95% accuracy guarantee and real-time email verification. Both are cheaper than smooth.AI's $147/mo starting price and deliver better data quality.

Can I get the same volume of contacts without smooth.AI?

Yes. Apollo's database has 270M+ contacts with better accuracy. ZoomInfo has 260M+ profiles. Even UpLead, the smallest database on this list, has 155M+ contacts. The difference is these tools don't pad their numbers with low-quality records. You'll get fewer bounces and more conversations from the same volume of exports.
